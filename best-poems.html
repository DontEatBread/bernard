Beneath the Blue

Beneath the blue, where dreams unfold, The sea is soft, the winds are bold. A whisper dances through the trees, A song of summer, carried on the breeze.

The sky, a canvas brushed with light, Embraces dawn, and fades to night. The stars are pearls that softly glow, Above the world, so far below.

Beneath the blue, the heart is free, In harmony with land and sea. A moment still, where time does cease, In nature’s arms, I find my peace.

Blossoms in the Breeze

Beneath the blossoms, soft and bright, The world is painted with delight. A thousand colors bloom in spring, As birds take flight on airy wing.

The breeze is light, the sky is clear, A melody the earth holds dear. Each petal, perfect, falls in grace, A fleeting moment in time’s embrace.

The garden hums, the flowers sway, In nature's breath, we drift away. For in the breeze, the soul finds peace, And in the blooms, all worries cease.

The Beauty of Being

In the quiet of the morning light, Beauty blooms, soft and bright. A moment lingers, pure and clear, The simple joy of being here.

The world is vast, yet so serene, In each small glance, a secret seen. Breathe the air, and you will find, That peace exists in heart and mind.

The beauty of being, simple, true, In every sunrise, in every hue. The wonders of life, both big and small, Whisper to us—this is the call.
